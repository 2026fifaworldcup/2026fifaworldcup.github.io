Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

This site is a static informational website about the 2026 World Cup. We do not ask visitors to create an account, submit personal information, log in, purchase anything, or leave comments on the site.

Information we do not collect directly

We do not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, ticket information, passport information, or private travel details. There are no registration forms, newsletter forms, member profiles, or checkout pages on this site.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is hosted as a public static website. Hosting providers and network infrastructure may automatically process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, referrer, device information, and timestamps for security, delivery, abuse prevention, and diagnostic purposes. We do not use this site to combine those logs with personal accounts because no user accounts exist here.

Analytics and advertising

When advertising or analytics services are enabled, third-party providers such as Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure performance, limit fraud, and prevent abuse. Those providers process data under their own privacy policies and may use information from this and other websites according to their settings and policies.

Google advertising information is available at Google Ads Technologies. Visitors can also review Google’s advertising controls and browser cookie settings if they want to manage personalized advertising or cookie behavior.

External links

Some pages may link to public tournament references, national federation information, or project contact pages. External websites are controlled by their own operators and are not covered by this privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Studio Nani through the contact page. Please do not include sensitive personal information in public GitHub Issues.
